Covid-19 Relief Funds


A novel coronavirus (CoV) is a new strain of coronavirus.

The disease caused by the novel coronavirus first identified in Wuhan, China, has been named coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) – ‘CO’ stands for corona, ‘VI’ for virus, and ‘D’ for disease. Formerly, this disease was referred to as ‘2019 novel coronavirus’ or ‘2019-nCoV.’

The COVID-19 virus is a new virus linked to the same family of viruses as Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS) and some types of common cold.



Ways Your Family Can Help Covid-19 Patients

SPONSOR

Let’s make sacrifices as a family to sponsor the Covid-19 Patients. Sponsoring them makes it possible for them to receive ongoing care in the form of food, clothing, medical care, shelter and a quality education to provide a foundation for their future.

GIVE

Let’s give.We can designate a jar for spare change. As a family, let’s donate the money to PM Care Fund. Let’s reach out to local shelters or hospitals care agencies to see if they are in need of medicines or PPE kit and mask … and then encourage them to help others.

Donate on your Birthday

When your big day is almost here, tell your friends and family that instead of gifts for yourself, you’ll be raising money for patients and needy ones. You can feed them and can help them recover from Covid-19. Setting aside your birthday for these people will make a meaningful impact in their lives.
